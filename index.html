Arctic Animals

Introduction

In all, the Arctic is home to more than 21,000 known species of highly cold-adapted mammals, birds,
fish, invertebrates, plants and fungi and microbe species. About 67 species of terrestrial mammals and 35 species of marine mammals occupy this biome, at least seasonally.

Types of Arctic Animals

There are many Arctic animals but in this case we will only be talking about a few,
such as the most endangered, the main animals, tundra animals, ect.
